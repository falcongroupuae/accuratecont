About Us

Accurate
Contracting

ABC

There is a tale behind every home, room, and building. We at Accurate Contracting are passionate about our work and dedicated to helping customers make spaces they enjoy.
We are not just a group of builders; we are partners on your trip. We're here to help you with everything from building a new house to remodeling your villa to arranging a modern interior to building a swimming pool. We'll make the process easy, smooth, and stress-free.
We are known as one of the most trustworthy construction businesses in Ajman and the United Arab Emirates because we put people first. For us, quality is important, deadlines are promises, and we take care of every job.

Our Story and What Makes Us Tick

The founders of Accurate Contracting have a simple idea: constructing should be fun, not stressful. We still do the same things we always have, but now we also offer building, villa refurbishment, interior fit-out, swimming pool construction, and MEP contracting. We are a team of builders, designers, engineers, and solvers who enjoy what we do. Seeing families delighted in their new homes, businesses pleased with their new interiors, and clients sure that their project is in good hands gives us the motivation to work hard.
We don't just finish projects; we also develop trust for the long term.

How We Make Your Dream Come True

Building Projects with Care and Accuracy

We are a trustworthy building company that can handle anything from modest homes to big businesses. We handle all the planning, supervision, coordination, and execution so you may have a smooth and foreseeable building process.

Changing Homes with Care

Your home should show who you are. Our skilled house remodeling contractors are experts at villa renovations, upgrades, modernizations, and full makeovers. We convert old places into beautiful, useful houses without any stress.

Making beautiful swimming pools

We are a trusted firm that builds swimming pools that fit your lifestyle precisely. We develop family pools, luxury pools, rooftop pools, infinity pools, and more. They are all built to last, be comfortable, and look good.

Doing MEP work that is smart and dependable

Functionality and safety are important. Our skilled MEP contracting business makes sure that your building's climate control, electrical systems, plumbing, and safety features all meet UAE regulations and work well.

Making the inside of your home feel like you

Your place should show who you are. We are one of the most reliable fit-out businesses in the UAE. We make interiors that are fashionable, useful, and comfortable to live in. We create settings that inspire, from designing homes to fitting out offices and businesses.
